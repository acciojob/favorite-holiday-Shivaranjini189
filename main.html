Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the City of Light. It is famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, romantic atmosphere, and world-class cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic symbol
  2. Louvre Museum – home of Mona Lisa
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Gothic masterpiece
  4. Champs-Élysées – famous avenue
  5. Montmartre – artistic hilltop area

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – buttery pastry
  • Macarons – sweet delights
  • Escargots – French delicacy
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes
  • Baguette – classic bread
