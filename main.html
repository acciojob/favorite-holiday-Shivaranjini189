Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the City of Light. It is famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, romantic atmosphere, and world-class cuisine. From iconic landmarks to charming streets, Paris offers an unforgettable experience for travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic symbol of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – home to thousands of artworks including Mona Lisa
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture
  4. Champs-Élysées – a famous avenue for shopping and strolling
  5. Montmartre – known for its artistic history and scenic views

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – a buttery, flaky pastry
  • Macarons – colorful and delicate sweet treats
  • Escargots – a traditional French delicacy
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings
  • Baguette – a classic French bread
